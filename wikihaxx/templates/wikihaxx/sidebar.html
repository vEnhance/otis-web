Notes for OTIS
  • Unit articles should be titled by the short name shown on the catalog and on the OTIS-WEB portal.
  • Use the header [unit SLUG][/unit]. (The slug is the natural part of the unit's filename, e.g. "equality".)
  • Use the header [diamond PK][/diamond] for pages about diamonds.
  • There is also a pair [generic][/generic] that makes an empty table.
  • You can add pairs using the syntax Key | Value within any of the four blocks above.
  • Articles generally follow title case.
