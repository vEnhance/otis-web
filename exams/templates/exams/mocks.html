Registering for GradeScope

You only need to do this once per year.

  1. Open gradescope.com.
  2. Create an account using your real name and any email.
  3. When asked for a student ID, type {{ student.pk }}.
  4. After the account is created, add the OTIS course using the entry key {{ semester.gradescope_key }}.
  5. You can enter anything you want for the school (e.g. MIT).

Submitting to GradeScope

In brief:

  1. The time limit is 4.5 hours.
  2. Like a real olympiad, submit solutions on the answer sheets.
  3. Submit to GradeScope. Be careful to submit the right papers to the right buckets (you will only submit for three of five problems).
  4. Please submit at least one page for all three problems on your test.

Check the syllabus for full details.
